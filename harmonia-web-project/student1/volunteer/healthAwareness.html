Project Description

This Campaign aims to educate individuals on mental health issues, coping strategies, and stress management. Volunteers will play a crucial role in creating a safe space for open discussions and mental health awareness.

Objectives

  • Raise awareness of mental health challenges
  • Distribute pamphlets on mental wellness
  • Provides free counseling sessions for attendees

Target Audience

Low-income individuals, people with limited access to mental health resources

Volunteer Roles

  • Organize information booths
  • Help facilitate wellness workshops and sessions
  • Support counseling professionals in one-on-one consultations

Impact

Reach 500 individuals with valuable mental health resources and provide 200 free consultations
