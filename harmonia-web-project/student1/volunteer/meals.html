Project Description

This program prepares and delivers nutritious meals to families experiencing food insecurity. Volunteers help with meal planning, cooking, packaging, and delivering meals directly to those in need.

Objectives

  • Reduce hunger in vulnerable communities
  • Provide nutritionally balanced meals to families in need
  • Educate about healthy eating habits through recipe cards and nutrition information

Target Audience

Families with food insecurity, elderly individuals, and those with limited access to nutritious food

Volunteer Roles

  • Package meals for distribution
  • Assist with meal preparation and cooking
  • Deliver meals to families and community centers
  • Help with inventory management and food sourcing

Impact

Provide over 1,000 nutrtious meals per month to families in need and improve food security in our community
