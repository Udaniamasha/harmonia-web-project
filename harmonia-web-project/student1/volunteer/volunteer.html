Volunteer Programs

Join our community of volunteers dedicated to improving health and wellbeing around the world. Explore these opportunities to make a meaningful difference in people's lives.

Mental Health First Aid

Become a certified mental health first aid responder and assist those in crisis. This 12-week program includes professional training from licensed therapist, role-playing scenarios, and hands-on experience at local crisis centers. Graduates receive an internationally recognized certification valid for 3 years.

Fitness for All Initiative

Help promote physical health by leading outdoor fitness classes and fun activites for all ages. We provide training in inclusive exercise methods, adaptive fitness techniques, and group leadership. Activities include walking groups, gentle yoga, strength training, and children's games. Volunteer receive CPR/First Aid certification and fitness instructor basics. Ideal for active individuals passionate about making fitness accessible to everyone.

Healthy Meals for Families

Volunteer to prepare and deliver nutritious meals to families facing food insecurity. Program includes training in food safety, nutrition basics, and meal planning on a budget. Volunteers work in teams to prepare meals in commercial kitchens, package items, and coordinate deliveries. Each volunteer helps prepare approximately 50-100 meals per session. We also offer nutrition education workshops for recipient families. Food handler certification provided.
