Project Description

This program offers free fitness classes such as yoga, stretching, and light aerobics to encourage physical activity. Volunteers will assist by leading classes and supporting participants during exercises.

Objectives

  • Promote physical fitness in local communities
  • Provide outdoor exercise options for all age groups
  • Foster a sense of well-being through group activities

Target Audience

All ages, with a focus on elderly individuals and those with limited mobility

Volunteer Roles

  • Lead warm-ups and cool-downs
  • Assist with participants during exercises
  • Ensure safety during activities

Impact

Improve physical health in 300+ individuals with weekly fitness sessions
